About Appointment Manager

Appointment Manager is a simple and efficient web application designed to help individuals and businesses manage their appointments with ease. Whether you’re running a salon, clinic, or consultancy, our system helps you schedule, organize, and track all your bookings in one place.

With a clean interface and useful features, Appointment Manager reduces missed appointments and saves time for both clients and staff.

  • Easy Booking: Schedule appointments quickly with a user-friendly form.
  • Automated Reminders: Stay updated with email or SMS notifications.
  • Calendar Sync: Manage and view all your bookings on one simple dashboard.
  • Responsive Design: Access your appointments anytime, from any device.
